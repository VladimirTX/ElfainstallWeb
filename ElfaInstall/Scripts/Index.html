Welcome to elfa® Installation Service
Let Us Do the Work for You!

We are proud to offer professional elfa installation services exclusively for customers of The Container Store®. Let us do the work for you! We'll come to your home and install your elfa - and you'll be enjoying your organized space in no time!

Our installation service is 30% of the regular price of your elfa purchase (or a minimum of $180.00) and includes the following:
  • Removal of up to two shelves and two closet rods per wall, plus related touch-ups with flat white paint.
  • Installation of all purchased elfa products.
  • Courteous installers who will clean up thoroughly when they are finished.
  • Delivery of your elfa is an option with installation for an additional fee.
  • All personal items must be removed from the space before the installer arrives.
